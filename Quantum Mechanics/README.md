# Quantum Mechanics

This folder contains notes, derivations, and worked problems from my independent study of quantum mechanics.

Topics covered to include but not limited to:
- Hilbert spaces and Dirac notation
- Linear algebra — eigenvalues, Hermitian operators
- Postulates of QM — observables, measurement
- Qubits
- Spin-1/2 systems and angular momentum
- Tensor products and multi-qubit systems
- Density matrix formalism
- Entanglement — Schmidt decomposition, Bell states
- Time evolution — Schrodinger and Heisenberg pictures
- Harmonic oscillator — ladder operators
- Time-independent perturbation theory

This list will be updated as I work through each section.
